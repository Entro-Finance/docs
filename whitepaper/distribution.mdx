# Token Distribution Strategy

## Distribution Timeline

The ENTRO token distribution follows a carefully planned schedule to ensure sustainable growth and fair distribution among all stakeholders.

## Vesting Schedules

### Team & Advisors (20%)
- **Cliff Period**: 12 months
- **Vesting Duration**: 36 months linear
- **Monthly Release**: ~1.85% of allocation

### Private Sale (5%)
- **Cliff Period**: 6 months  
- **Vesting Duration**: 12 months linear
- **Monthly Release**: ~8.33% of allocation

### Community Rewards (30%)
- **Distribution**: 4 years linear
- **Purpose**: Liquidity mining, governance participation, ecosystem growth
