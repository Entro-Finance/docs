# Scalability Solutions

## Multi-Chain Architecture

Entro Finance is designed from the ground up to operate seamlessly across multiple blockchain networks, ensuring optimal performance and user experience regardless of network congestion.

We leverage various Layer 2 solutions to provide:

Our scalability infrastructure aims to achieve:
- **10,000+ TPS**: Transactions per second across all chains
- **Sub-second finality**: Near-instant transaction confirmation
- **99.9% uptime**: Reliable service availability
