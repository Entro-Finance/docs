# The Problem

While crypto continues to grow worldwide, real-world usability remains limited. Many platforms force users through complicated processes, wallet connections, or restrictions that leave funds locked or vulnerable. Traditional payment rails don't integrate smoothly with digital assets, which prevents crypto from achieving its potential as a true medium of exchange.
