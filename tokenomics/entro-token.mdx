# ENTRO Tokenomics

## Token Benefits

### For Card Users

**Earn more by holding ENTRO:**
- **Funding Bonus**: Get 2-5% extra value when funding cards (fund with BTC, ETH, SOL, USDC)
- **Cashback Rewards**: Higher cashback rates for ENTRO holders
- **Priority Features**: Early access to new products
- **Exclusive Benefits**: Access to card tiers and special perks

*Note: Cards are funded with crypto (BTC, ETH, SOL, USDC, etc). ENTRO is held for benefits, not spent.*

## Value Drivers

### Creator Fees Buyback

15-20% of all platform revenue is automatically used to buy back ENTRO tokens from the market, creating consistent demand and supporting token value.

### Revenue Sharing

ENTRO stakers earn a share of:
- Card transaction fees
- Merchant processing fees
- Premium subscription revenue
- Partner integration fees

### Value Mechanics

- Creator fees buyback (15-20% of protocol revenue)
- Systematic market purchases create demand
- Growing utility creates natural demand
- Limited supply with expanding use cases

## How It Works

### Smart Buybacks

Our automated system allocates 15-20% of platform revenue to buy back ENTRO tokens from the market, creating consistent buying pressure and value support.

## Card Tiers

Access different card tiers based on your ENTRO holdings:
- **Standard**: Entry-level card tier
- **Premium**: Enhanced features and limits
- **High Tier**: Premium benefits and higher limits
- **Max Tier**: Ultimate card experience
