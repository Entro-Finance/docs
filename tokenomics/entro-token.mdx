# ENTRO Tokenomics

The ENTRO token powers the Entro Finance ecosystem, rewarding users and driving platform growth through innovative tokenomics.

## Token Basics

**ENTRO** is built on Solana as an SPL token, providing fast transactions and low fees. Total supply is capped, ensuring long-term value stability.

## Token Distribution

**Fair and balanced allocation:**
- **Community & Rewards**: 35% for users and growth
- **Ecosystem Fund**: 25% for partnerships and development  
- **Team & Advisors**: 15% with long-term vesting
- **Liquidity**: 15% for market stability
- **Treasury**: 10% for future initiatives

All team tokens have multi-year vesting to ensure long-term alignment.

## Token Benefits

### For Card Users

**Earn more by holding ENTRO:**
- **Funding Bonus**: Get 2-5% extra when funding with ENTRO
- **Fee Discounts**: Reduce or eliminate transaction fees
- **Cashback Rewards**: Higher cashback rates for ENTRO holders
- **Priority Features**: Early access to new products

### Staking Rewards

**Lock ENTRO to earn more:**

- Higher rewards for longer lock periods
- Compound your earnings automatically
- Participate in exclusive opportunities

### Governance Power

**Shape the future of Entro:**
- Vote on protocol upgrades

## Value Drivers

### Buyback Program

A portion of all platform revenue automatically buys back ENTRO tokens, creating consistent demand and reducing supply.

### Revenue Sharing

ENTRO stakers earn a share of:
- Card transaction fees
- Merchant processing fees
- Premium subscription revenue
- Partner integration fees

### Deflationary Mechanics

- Regular token burns from fees
- Decreasing emission schedule
- Growing utility creates natural demand
- Limited supply with expanding use cases

## How It Works

### Smart Buybacks

Our automated system uses platform revenue to buy ENTRO tokens from the market weekly, creating consistent demand and price support.

### Staking Tiers

**Choose your staking strategy:**
- **Flexible**: No lock period, moderate rewards
- **Bronze**: 30-day lock, higher APY
- **Silver**: 90-day lock, premium rewards  
- **Gold**: 180-day lock, exclusive benefits
- **Platinum**: 365-day lock, maximum returns

Longer commitments earn significantly higher rewards.
