# Token Distribution Model

The ENTRO token distribution is designed to ensure long-term sustainability and fair allocation across all stakeholders.

## Total Supply

**1,000,000,000 ENTRO** tokens (fixed supply, no inflation)

## Distribution Breakdown

### Public Sale - 35% (350,000,000 ENTRO)

**Launch Details**:
- Platform: Pump.fun on Solana
- No presale or private rounds before public
- Fair launch mechanism
- 100% of tokens available immediately

### Ecosystem Fund - 25% (250,000,000 ENTRO)

**Purpose**:
- Liquidity provision
- Partnership incentives
- Community rewards
- Marketing campaigns

**Vesting**: 48 months linear release

### Team & Advisors - 15% (150,000,000 ENTRO)

**Allocation**:
- Core team: 12%
- Advisors: 3%

**Vesting**:
- 12 month cliff
- 36 month linear vesting after cliff
- Smart contract enforced

### Liquidity Pool - 15% (150,000,000 ENTRO)

**Usage**:
- Initial DEX liquidity
- Market making
- Price stability

**Lock Period**: 6 months minimum

### Treasury - 10% (100,000,000 ENTRO)

**Governance**:
- DAO controlled after 6 months
- Community proposals required
- Multi-sig security

## Emission Schedule

Monthly token releases follow a predictable schedule to avoid market shocks.

## Token Burns

Buyback and burn mechanism reduces total supply over time:
- 50% of protocol fees used for buybacks
- Burned tokens sent to null address
- Deflationary pressure on supply
