# Token Distribution Model

The ENTRO token distribution is designed to ensure long-term sustainability and fair allocation across all stakeholders.

## Token Architecture

Fixed supply model with programmatic distribution through smart contracts.

## Distribution Framework

### Public Distribution

**Launch Mechanism**  
Fair launch protocol ensuring equal access to initial token distribution

### Ecosystem Development

**Purpose**  
Protocol development, liquidity bootstrapping, and ecosystem growth initiatives

**Release Schedule**  
Programmatic vesting through time-locked contracts

### Team Allocation

**Structure**  
Long-term alignment through extended vesting periods

**Vesting Parameters**  
Cliff period followed by linear distribution

### Protocol Liquidity

**Function**  
Initial liquidity provision and market depth establishment

**Management**  
Automated market making through algorithmic strategies

### Treasury Reserve

**Governance**:
- DAO controlled after 6 months
- Community proposals required
- Multi-sig security

## Emission Schedule

Monthly token releases follow a predictable schedule to avoid market shocks.

## Creator Fees Buyback

Protocol implements systematic buyback mechanism:
- **15-20% of all protocol revenue** allocated for token buybacks
- Automatic market purchases create consistent demand
- Purchased tokens distributed to stakers and ecosystem
- Transparent on-chain execution
