# ENTRO Token Utility

The ENTRO token serves multiple essential functions within the Entro Finance ecosystem, creating value for holders while powering platform operations.

## Core Utilities

### 1. Card Funding Benefits

ENTRO holders receive preferential treatment when funding cards:

| Funding Method | Standard Rate | ENTRO Holder Bonus |
|----------------|---------------|--------------------|
| USDC | 1:1 | 1:1 |
| SOL | Market rate | Market rate |
| ENTRO | Market rate | Market rate + 2% |
| Other SPL tokens | Market rate - 0.5% | Market rate |

### Tiered Bonuses

Additional bonuses based on ENTRO holdings:

- **Bronze (1,000+ ENTRO)**: 2% funding bonus
- **Silver (10,000+ ENTRO)**: 3% funding bonus
- **Gold (50,000+ ENTRO)**: 4% funding bonus
- **Platinum (100,000+ ENTRO)**: 5% funding bonus

### 2. Fee Reduction

ENTRO holders enjoy reduced fees across all platform services:

| Service | Standard Fee | Bronze | Silver | Gold | Platinum |
|---------|-------------|--------|--------|------|----------|
| Card Creation | $5 | $4.50 | $3.75 | $2.50 | Free |
| Monthly Maintenance | $2 | $1.80 | $1.50 | $1 | Free |
| ATM Withdrawal | $2.50 | $2.25 | $2 | $1.50 | $1 |
| International Transaction | 1% | 0.9% | 0.75% | 0.5% | 0.25% |

### 3. Governance Rights

ENTRO tokens grant voting power in protocol governance:

**Voting Power Calculation**:
- 1 ENTRO = 1 base vote
- Staked tokens receive 1.5x multiplier
- Long-term staking (180+ days) receives 2x multiplier

**Governance Decisions**:
- Protocol parameter adjustments
- Fee structure modifications
- Treasury allocation
- Partnership approvals
- Feature prioritization
