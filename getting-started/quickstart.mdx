# Quick Start Guide

<img src="/assets/gif1.gif" alt="Entro Finance Card Creation Process" width="450" />

Get your first Entro Finance card and start spending crypto in under 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- Crypto to fund your card (BTC, ETH, USDC, etc.)
- Valid email address
- Basic personal information for card creation

## Step 1: Visit Entro Finance

Go to [entrofinanec.com/onboarding](http://entrofinanec.com/onboarding) to start creating your crypto card.

## Step 2: Choose Your Card Details

### Select Card Type
- **Virtual Card**: Instant creation for online use
- **Physical Card**: Delivered to your address

### Set Your Funding Amount
- Choose how much crypto you want to load (e.g., $150 worth of BTC)
- Select your preferred cryptocurrency

## Step 3: Complete the Transaction

1. **Get Payment Address**: The system provides a crypto address
2. **Send Your Crypto**: Transfer the exact amount to the provided address
3. **Confirmation**: Your card gets funded automatically once the transaction confirms

## Step 4: Start Spending

### Online Purchases
1. Use your card details at checkout
2. Enter card number, expiry, and CVV
3. Complete purchase instantly

### In-Store Purchases
1. Activate your physical card when it arrives
2. Set your 4-digit PIN
3. Tap, insert, or swipe at any merchant

## Step 5: Manage Your Card

**Real-time controls available:**
- Freeze/unfreeze instantly
- Set spending limits
- View transaction history
- Set merchant restrictions
- Enable/disable international use

## Security Features

- **Protection**: Enterprise-grade security protocols
- **Monitoring**: Real-time fraud detection
- **Controls**: Instant card management
- **Notifications**: Transaction alerts via SMS/email

## Advanced Features

**Multi-Card Management**: Create separate cards for different purposes (travel, business, personal) with individual limits and controls.

**Transaction Monitoring**: Get real-time notifications via SMS or email for all card activity.

**Global Compatibility**: Use your cards in 190+ countries with automatic currency conversion.

## Security Best Practices

- Never share your card details with unauthorized parties
- Set appropriate spending limits for your usage
- Enable transaction notifications for real-time monitoring
- Regularly review your transaction history
