## Core Architecture

![How Entro Finance Works](/assets/howEntroWorks.png)

Our system consists of three main layers:

### 1. Blockchain Foundation
**Built on Solana for speed and efficiency**
- Lightning-fast transaction processing
- Minimal fees for all operations
- Proven security and reliability

### 2. Card Management System
**Your crypto becomes instantly spendable**
- Virtual cards created in seconds
- Physical cards delivered globally
- Real-time controls and monitoring

### 3. Payment Processing
**Works everywhere traditional cards work**
- Automatic crypto-to-fiat conversion
- Instant authorization and settlement
- Global merchant acceptance

## How Transactions Work

**Simple 4-step process:**

1. **You spend** using your Entro card anywhere
2. **We verify** your crypto balance instantly
3. **We convert** your crypto to the required currency
4. **Merchant receives** payment immediately

All of this happens in under half a second.

## Security Features

**Multi-layered protection:**
- Smart contract audits by leading security firms
- Real-time fraud monitoring
- Instant card controls (freeze, limits, restrictions)
- Biometric authentication support
- Enterprise-grade security protocols

## For Users

**Simple and powerful:**
- No monthly fees or hidden charges
- Spend crypto anywhere Visa/Mastercard work
- Real-time notifications and controls
- Global acceptance in 180+ countries

## For Merchants

**Better than traditional processing:**
- Lower fees than standard card processing
- Instant settlement with no chargebacks
- Global customer reach
- Simple integration process
