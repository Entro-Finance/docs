<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial Launch">
    - API integration for non-reloadable cards
    - Seamless online and in-person spending experience
    - Introduction of the $ENTRO token on Solana
    - Buyback-driven rewards for token holders
  </Step>

  <Step title="Expansion Phase">
    - Development of reloadable cards, supported by platform revenue
    - Broader merchant and regional coverage
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ecosystem Growth">
    - Launch of a browser extension for simple asset management and payments
    - Rollout of a mobile app for on-the-go control, including card management and real-time balance visibility
  </Step>
</Steps>
